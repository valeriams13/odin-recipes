Oatmeal

Description

This dish is one of my all-time favorite breakfast recipes. The reason is because it is really easy to make, and not to mention it fills me up before a long day at work or school. I make it in a microwave to make it extra easy. Also, it is a nutritious breakfast filled with healthy carbohydrates, protein, fats, and vitamins from the fruit. It's so yummy!

I hope you try it out, enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups of oatmeal
  • Water or milk (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp of peanut butter
  • Fruit (to taste, can be frozen or fresh. My favorite are berries or bananas!)
  • Sweetener to taste (I like stevia)
  • A pinch of salt
  • A sprinkle of cinnamon

Steps

  1. First, you will put your oats and the water/milk into a microwave-safe bowl, along with the sweetener, salt, cinnamon, and peanut butter. Make sure to mix it all in.
  2. Now, put it into the microwave for around 1 minute and a half. Take it out, stir it, and put it in for 1 more minute. I like doing this because I feel it makes it creamier and thicker.
  3. When you take it out, you can go ahead and add the fruit, and/or more peanut butter if you would like. It is that simple!
