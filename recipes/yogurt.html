Yogurt with Berries and Granola

Description

This is another easy-to-make recipe, which is refreshing and delicious as well as high in protein and vitamins.

Hope you get to try it out!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup of greek yogurt
  • Granola to taste
  • Sweetener (to taste, I prefer stevia)
  • Fruit of your choice

Steps

  1. Put your yogurt in a bowl along with the sweetener, and mix it in.
  2. Simply add your fruit and your granola on top of the yogurt, and your delicious breakfast is done!
