French Toast

Description

This breakfast recipe takes a little more time to make, but it is still relativaly easy. This is delicious and perfect for a cold morning, and it is very filling!

Make sure you try this one out!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • A splash of milk
  • A splash of vanilla extract
  • Sweetener of choice (I chose stevia)
  • Butter/oil
  • 2 slices of bread (or more if you would like)
  • Fruit (to taste)
  • Maple syrup (to taste)
  • Optional - 1 tbsp peanut butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, crack the egg and add the milk, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and the sweetener. Mix it well.
  2. Coat your pan with some butter or oil, and heat it up.
  3. Dip the slices of bread into your egg mixture, and add them into the pan.
  4. Wait a few minutes until one side of the bread is golden, and then flip it.
  5. Once both sides are golden brown, add them to a plate.
  6. Once in the plate, add your fruit and syrup, as well as the peanut butter if you would like, and you are finished!
